Summary

On February 14th, I posted a link to social media containing 5 downloadable .png files of illustrated Valentines day cards. Each of them a in a unique design portraying the complexity and uniqueness of love. The illustrations are meant to evoke emotions, memories and the essence of one’s relationship.

5 ways to express love

Bunny love

a cutesy way to show affection

Blossoming love

for relationships who’ve been through thick and thin together and are excited to grow side by side mentally and physically

a unique way to show affection for a lover
