Increasing user data collection potential and personalizing app at the same time

The App

European Coffee Trip

Roles

UX designer UX researcher

Timeline

Approx. 2 months

Tools

Figma
Google forms
MS Excel

Summary

During the process of learning Figma by copying the European Coffee Trip, an idea turned up to add cafe reviews. However, this was dismissed by user research, The user research revealed that many users are regular travelers and use the app while traveling. So instead, I designed a feature that allows users to save memories from their coffee trips in the form of photos.

Key takeaways

Users’ wants don’t mean the feature will be used

In research a user said they’d appreciate a review feature.
However, to a “How did you react when you encountered a bad coffee service?” question, none of the users answered they had left a review or made a comment to staff.

A feature both business and users can gain from

The new special feature design aligns both business’ and users’ wants and needs.
One sollution gives users a personalised app full of their own memories from travels and to business owners a way to track their target customers.

Current search for speacialty coffee

Simple app, yet downloaded by many.
European Coffee Trip app functions as an encyclopedia of specialty coffee cafes. The current app is mostly financed by listed cafes that can buy a subscription in order to be featured in the app.
The user can discover nearby cafes and filter them based on their liking.

Final design

Discover the prototype

No annoying waiting to load

Current app doesn’t have a loading screen. A throbber appears on the screen while loading the app. In order to reduce users’ annoyance with a throbber a new loading screen in the form of a coffee cup was designed.

Keeping memories from coffee trips

New feature of adding coffee memories from trips was added to the menu. The trips are sorted according to cities and cafes. Memories are kept in the form of photos with a description similar to the Instagram feed. A user gains a familiarity and fondness for the app, while business owners gain a way to keep track of frequency of cafe visits.

Easily available button

So that the new feature is conveniently available, a new button was added to a cafe page. The button replaces a navigation button as there are already 2 on a cafe page.
